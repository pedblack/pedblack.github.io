WEDDING PARTY

Once upon a time, in a land of frost and snow, two southern european souls crossed paths. Aurore, a French writer, and Pedro, a Portuguese musician, who both worked undercover as a researcher and an engineer, respectively.

It all happened one night. Looking for the secrets of the Universe in beverages famous for enlightening the mind while sharpening the senses, Aurore and Pedro were, unknowingly, about to meet. The music was loud but their hearts beat stronger as their eyes found each other across the room. With a welcoming smile she invited him over. Cutting through the crowd, as a Portuguese navigator braving the seas, he came closer, tried a joke and they shared a laugh that will never end.

Now they have decided to celebrate their bond with family and friends.

They count on you to make this event magical!

FAQ

When should I arrive and how long should I stay?

Most of the events kick off on Friday night and end on Sunday afternoon, but please enjoy the area for as long as you're able.

Any tips on what should I wear?

Weather permitting, the ceremony and parts of the reception will be outdoors, so shoes that can traverse gravel and grass would make your life easier.

Dress code: Elegant.

Should I pack anything special?

The wedding venue has a swimming pool so, if the weather is right and you would like to take a swim on Sunday, pack a bathing suit.

What is the weather like in the region this time of year?

In the beginning of September, Cintegabelle averages a high of 24°C and a low of 14°C. For a more detailed view check this website

Will you be accommodating any diet restrictions?

We will accommodatingmodate vegetarian meals so please let us know in the RSVP if that is your preferred choice. If you have any other dietary concerns, feel free to let us know so we can explore options for you!
